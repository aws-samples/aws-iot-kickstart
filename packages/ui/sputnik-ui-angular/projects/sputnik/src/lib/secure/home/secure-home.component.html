Benefits and Features

Get started with a few clicks

Start connecting and monitoring your trucks, boats, factories with just a few clicks. No need to worry about what is happening under the hood.

Easy to use interface

Provides the ability to easily launch IoT Systems from a variety of templates you define all within the comforts of a web-based GUI.

Monitor and observe

Once your devices have connected and have started sending data, you can monitor individual devices from the console or view them as part of systems or groups.

Use it out-of-the-box or customize it

Open-Source, you can use out of the box. Deploy a new stack and customize the code to meet your specific business requirements. Even better, contribute to the community by adding templates to the git repository.
